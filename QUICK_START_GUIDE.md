# Quick Start Guide: Product Comparison & Quote Builder

## 🎯 Quick Overview

### Product Comparison
Compare up to 4 generator products side-by-side with detailed specifications, export options, and sharing capabilities.

### Quote Builder
Create professional quotes with custom pricing, discounts, and customer information.

---

## 📊 Using Product Comparison

### Step 1: Select Products
1. Go to **Products** page
2. Browse the DG Sets catalog
3. Click **"Add to Compare"** on any product card
4. A floating bar appears at the bottom showing selected products

### Step 2: Compare
1. Select at least 2 products (up to 4 maximum)
2. Click **"Compare Now"** in the floating bar
3. View side-by-side comparison with all specifications

### Step 3: Export & Share
- **Export to CSV**: Download spreadsheet for analysis
- **Export to PDF**: Generate professional report
- **Print**: Print-friendly layout
- **Share**: Copy link to clipboard or use native share

### Tips
- Remove individual products by clicking the X button
- Clear all products with "Clear All" button
- Products persist across sessions (saved in browser)
- Mobile-friendly responsive design

---

## 💼 Using Quote Builder

### Step 1: Customer Information
1. Navigate to **Quote Builder** from:
   - Navbar → "Quote Builder"
   - Compare page → "Create a Quote" card
   - Compare bar → "Quote" button
2. Fill in customer details:
   - Name (required)
   - Email (required)
   - Company, Phone, Address (optional)

### Step 2: Add Products
1. Click the **"Select a product to add"** dropdown
2. Choose products from your catalog
3. Each product appears as a card with:
   - Product image and details
   - Quantity selector
   - Unit price input
   - Discount percentage
   - Item notes field

### Step 3: Configure Pricing
For each product:
- Set **Quantity** (default: 1)
- Enter **Unit Price** in ₹
- Apply **Discount** (0-100%)
- Add **Item Notes** (optional)

### Step 4: Quote Details
1. Add **Quote Notes** (payment terms, delivery, warranty)
2. Set **Quote Validity** (default: 30 days)
3. Review the **Summary Panel**:
   - Subtotal
   - Total Discount (in green)
   - After Discount
   - GST (18%)
   - **Final Total**

### Step 5: Generate & Send
- **Download PDF**: Generate quote document
- **Send via Email**: Email quote to customer
- **Save Draft**: Save for later editing
- **Load Draft**: Restore saved quote

### Tips
- Save drafts frequently to avoid losing work
- Use item notes for specifications or customizations
- Quote validity helps create urgency
- All calculations update in real-time

## 💾 Data Persistence

### What's Saved
- **Comparison**: Selected product IDs
- **Quote Draft**: All quote data (items, customer, notes)

### Where It's Saved
- Browser LocalStorage (client-side only)
- Persists across sessions
- Cleared when browser data is cleared

### What's NOT Saved
- Generated PDFs (download only)
- Sent emails (one-time action)
- Comparison exports (download only)

## ❓ Troubleshooting

### Products not appearing in comparison
- Check if products are published
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache

### Quote calculations incorrect
- Verify unit prices are entered
- Check discount percentages (0-100)
- Ensure quantities are positive numbers

### Draft not loading
- Check browser LocalStorage is enabled
- Verify draft was saved successfully
- Try saving a new draft

### Export not working
- Check browser allows downloads
- Disable popup blockers
- Try different export format
